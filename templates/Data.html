About Our Data
Nutrek generates all its reports based on the United States Department of Agriculture's (USDA) Branded Food Products Database (BFPD).

About Data

USDA Branded Food Product Database
Data Source: According to the USDA, their database was created by a Public-Private Partnership aiming to “enhance public health” and “sharing of open data” of branded foods and private label data. The database includes branded products listed by Nutrient Database Number (NDB) and each product's corresponding nutritional values, serving size information, and ingredients list. The database is frequently updated when new information is received and processed. Data used in the BFPD download file is current as of July 13, 2018. Date Downloaded: 10/08/19
Publisher: USDA Agricultural Research Services
Authors: Pehrsson, Pamela R.,Haytowitz, David B., McKillop, Kyle A., Moore, G., Finley, John W.,Fukagawa, Naomi K.
Time period, geography, and/or scope covered:
United States of America

Overview of Dataset
USDA Branded Food Products Database - ASCII csv file, 07/13/2018, BFPD_csv_07132018.zip, BFD_Doc.pdf, Serving_size.csv, Serving size file, Products.csv, Products file, Nutrients.csv, Nutrients file, Derivation_Code_Description.csv, Derivations Code Description file.
