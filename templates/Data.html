About Our Data
Nutrek generates all its reports based on the United States Department of Agriculture's (USDA) Branded Food Products Database (BFPD).

About Data



About Our Data Source
According to the USDA, their Branded Food Products database was created by a Public-Private Partnership aiming to “enhance public health” and “sharing of open data” of branded foods and private label data. The database includes branded products listed by Nutrient Database Number (NDB) and each product's corresponding nutritional values, serving size information, and ingredients list. The database is frequently updated when new information is received and processed. Nutrek uses a BFPD data file that is current as of July 13, 2018.

Modification to data
Our main commitment is providing more general information on nutrients per serving size, ingredients, and helping those with food allergies stay safe and be informed about what is in the foods they want to eat. We only used the following in our cleaned up data set:
- product name
- nutrients on the Nutrition Facts Panel per serving size and 100 gram-basis, 100 ml-basis, or fluid oz-basis, ingredient list, (never before captured by USDA)
and excluded serving size in grams or milliliters, manufacturer, and date stamp associated with the most current product formulation.
Allergens and ingredient breakdown only require ingredients in each product, while nutrient breakdown only involves nutrients per serving amount.
The official product name (without manufacturer) is used to identify food product.
